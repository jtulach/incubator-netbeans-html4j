Tic Tac Toe 3

What is this?

This is a rewrite of React.js Tic Tac Toe tutorial controlled by application written in JavaFX Light and transpiled to JavaScript.

Action

Click on any square to change its state to X. However this time the Square notifies the Board of an onClick event and let's the Board change its BoardState (with List<Character> and identification of next player) and re-render the whole board.
